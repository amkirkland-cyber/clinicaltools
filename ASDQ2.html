Autism Symptom Dimensions Questionnaire (ASDQ)

Parent/Caregiver form — 39 items — 5-point Likert (Never → Very often) plus Not applicable — scored 0–4

Instructions. Please rate how often the child does each behavior. If an item does not apply (e.g., too young, non-speaking, or insufficient information), choose Not applicable. Scores and subscale averages exclude Not applicable responses.

Subscales (DSM-5 terms)
  • Basic social communication (DSM-5 A2): items 1, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10, 11
  • Affiliation / social motivation (DSM-5 A1/A3): items 2, 3, 15
  • Perspective taking / reciprocity (DSM-5 A1): items 7, 12, 13, 14
  • Peer relationships (DSM-5 A3): items 9, 16, 17
  • Repetitive motor behavior (DSM-5 B1): items 18, 19, 20, 21
  • Sensory interests (DSM-5 B4): items 29, 30, 31
  • Insistence on sameness (DSM-5 B2): items 22, 23, 24, 25
  • Sensory sensitivities (DSM-5 B4): items 26, 27, 28
  • Restricted interests (DSM-5 B3): items 32, 33, 34, 35

Items 36–39 contribute to the total score only (not assigned to a specific subscale in the published CFA model).
