Reference (RFs / Diet)

Risk factors (RFs) & risk conditions (RCs)
FHx (+): MI/angina/CABG/stent/angioplasty/sudden cardiac death in parent/grandparent/aunt/uncle: male <55, female <65.
High-level RFs
  • Hypertension requiring drug therapy (BP ≥99th%ile + 5 mmHg)
  • Current cigarette smoker
  • BMI ≥97th%ile
  • Presence of high-risk conditions (below)
Moderate-level RFs
  • Hypertension not requiring drug therapy
  • BMI ≥95th%ile and <97th%ile
  • HDL-C <40 mg/dL
  • Presence of moderate-risk conditions (below)
High-risk conditions
  • Diabetes mellitus (Type 1 or Type 2)
  • Homozygous familial hypercholesterolemia (hoFH)
  • Chronic/end-stage renal disease or post-renal transplant
  • Kawasaki disease with persistent coronary aneurysms/stenoses/occlusions
  • Orthotopic heart transplantation
Moderate-risk conditions
  • Familial hypercholesterolemia (FH)
  • Kawasaki disease with regressed coronary aneurysms
  • Chronic inflammatory disease (juvenile rheumatoid arthritis; SLE)
  • HIV
  • Nephrotic syndrome
CHILD-1 (baseline diet)
  • Total fat: 25–30% daily kcal
  • Saturated fat: 8–10% daily kcal
  • Avoid trans fats
  • Mono- and polyunsaturated fat: up to 20% daily kcal
  • Cholesterol: <300 mg/day
  • Encourage high dietary fiber intake
  • Activity: ≥1 hour/day moderate-to-vigorous (children >5y)
  • Primary beverages: fat-free unflavored milk and water
  • Limit/avoid sugar-sweetened beverages; limit 100% juice to ≤4 oz/day
CHILD-2 (your handout) – numeric targets
  • Total fat: 25–30% daily calories
  • Saturated fat: ≤7% daily calories
  • Monounsaturated fat: ~10% daily calories
  • Avoid trans fats
  • Cholesterol: <200 mg/day
  • Activity: ≥1 hour/day moderate-to-vigorous
  • Screen time: <2 hours/day
  • Omega-3 supplement: 1–4 g/day
  • Psyllium: 6 g/day (2–12y); 12 g/day (≥12y)
  • Plant stanols/sterols: up to 2 g/day
